        Beckhoff in a nutshell

        Familiarize yourself with our innovations in our brief, easy-to-understand Highlight Tour. Register for our tour at Hannover Messe and receive an overview of our wide range of automation products in just 30 minutes.

        Learn more

        Short set-up and changeover times thanks to a high degree of automation

        Durrer Spezialmaschinen AG in Switzerland relies on PC-based control from Beckhoff for its production system for photo calendars. A total of 93 axes, run by Beckhoff servo drives and servomotors and controlled by our Beckhoff TwinCAT automation software, execute sophisticated process sequences.

        Learn more

        MTP is the future

        How flexible is your production? With the Module Type Package (MTP), you can make your processes future-proof and your systems adaptable. Discover all the possibilities in our white paper.

        Learn more

        Control hardware with a small footprint with a 40% cost reduction

        Lehnen, a US company, relies on compact control, networking, and servo technology from Beckhoff to further develop their filling system for lip balm into a standard product.

        Learn more

        Flexibly scalable hardware and software for a modular system

        Byosis, a Dutch company, found the right automation solution for their modular system for sustainable fertilizer production in PC-based control from Beckhoff.
