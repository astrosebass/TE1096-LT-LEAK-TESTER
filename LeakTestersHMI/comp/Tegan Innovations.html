3

Pilot Line

The Pilot line concept is a new and exciting production solution offered to the medical device and pharmaceutical Industries for the production of small volumes of new technology.

It is a flexible platform designed to be a modular system, which can be adapted quickly as the product evolves in its early life cycle.
This gives our clients the advantage of getting their product to the marketplace very quickly.

For more information on this line please reach out to our Senior Pilot Line Development Engineer Maurice Foley on 051 334567
